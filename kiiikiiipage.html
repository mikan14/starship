KiiKii

KiiKii is a South Korean girl group formed by Starship Entertainment. They officially debuted on March 24, 2025, with the EP Uncut Gem and the title track "I Do Me"

The group consists of five members: Jiyu, Leesol, Sui, Haum, and Kya. Known for their experimental sound, unique visuals, and bold stage presence, KiiKii made a strong impression with their debut, earning their first music show win on Show! Music Core just two weeks later.

The name KiiKii combines the Korean onomatopoeia “kiki” (a giggle) with the English word “key”, symbolizing joy and the idea of unlocking one's true self.
