STARSHIP Audition

Finding a Future With Starship Entertainment

Recruitment Field

Vocal / Rap / Dance / Appearance


Recruitment process

Application through website and mail → Individual notice for first round of successful applicants → Camera test and audition by person in charge → Final audition

Mailing address: 06070 Starship ENT Newcomer Development Team, 4-5, Samseong-ro 146-gil, Gangnam-gu, Seoul


How to Apply

Click the 'Apply for Audition' button below and fill out the application form

Required Attachments:


1) A total of 2 files of frontal close-up and full-body photos


2) Video and audio files according to the support field


Vocal/Rap - Free song, video or voice file of 1 minute or longer


Dance - Free song, video file of 1 minute or longer


Appearance - Free-form video file of 30 seconds or more (acting, self-introduction, walking, posing, etc.)


Note

Photos that have been edited using Photoshop or filters, or photos that are not taken from the front will be excluded from the first round of judging.


Video files that are out of focus or have poor audio and video quality may be disqualified from the review process.

FAQ

Q When will I receive notification that I have passed?


Only successful applicants will be individually notified within 2-3 weeks.


Q Is team support available?


Duets and teams are not accepted.

Team applications will be disqualified during the screening process, so please apply individually.


Q I applied for a previous audition. Can I reapply?


Yes, you can.

You can apply through the website or by mail, attaching the documents in the same way as domestic applicants.


Q Are there any plans for another open audition?


The detailed schedule has not been decided yet, but we are planning to meet you again thanks to the enthusiastic support you have given us for this open audition, so please look forward to it.


Until the next open audition, you can always apply for the audition through the website and by mail.


Q Can't people under 14 years old apply for an audition?


Unfortunately, applicants under the age of 14 cannot apply for auditions through the website or by mail.
