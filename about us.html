ABOUT STARSHIP

Starship Entertainment was founded with the mission of suggesting a new direction for the popular culture industry and discovering and nurturing talented popular artists. Since then, it has produced talented artists with star quality and has been attracting attention through various attempts and marketing.

Starship Entertainment Co., Ltd. is engaged in various entertainment businesses with singer management and actor management as its two mainstays .

STARSHIP ENTERTAINMENT

Starship is a spaceship that holds stars, and it contains the will to lead the advancement of entertainment based on a creative mind and endless passion , and it expresses the appearance of not settling for reality and constantly challenging new areas.
