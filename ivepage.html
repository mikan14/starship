IVE

IVE is a South Korean girl group formed by Starship Entertainment. They officially devbuted on December 1, 2021, with the single "ELEVEN"

The group consists of six members: Yujin, Gaeul, Rei, Wonyoung, Liz, and Leeseo. Known for their confident, elegant image and strong visuals, IVE quickly rose to fame with hits like "LOVE DIVE", "AFTER LIKE", and "I AM".

The name IVE stands for "I HAVE", reflecting their bold concept of self-assuredness and completeness
