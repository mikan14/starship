CRAVITY

CRAVITY is a South Korean boy group formed by Starship Entertainment. They officially debuted on April 14, 2020, with the mini-album Season 1. Hideout: Remember Who We Are and the title track "Break All The Rules".

The group consists of nine members: Serim, Allen, Jungmo, Woobin, Wonjin, Minhee, Hyeongjun, Taeyoung, and Seongmin. Known for their powerful performances, trendy visuals, and high-energy music, CRAVITY quickly rose to attention as Monster Rookies.

The name CRAVITY combines "Creativity" and "Gravity," symbolizing the group’s pull toward fans and their desire to create a new universe together.
