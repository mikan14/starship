WJSN (Cosmic Girls)

WJSN is a South Korean-Chinese girl group formed by Starship Entertainment and Yuehua Entertainment. They officially debuted on February 25, 2016, with the mini album Would You Like? featuring the title tracks “Mo Mo Mo” and “Catch Me.”

Originally consisting of 12 members, with 3 more added later (including Chinese members), the group is known for their cosmic/fantasy concept, powerful performances, and strong discography with hits like “Secret”, “Save Me, Save You”, and “As You Wish.”

The name WJSN (Cosmic Girls) reflects their celestial theme and diverse charms across units like Wonder, Joy, Sweet, and Natural.
