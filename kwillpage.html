K.Will

K.Will (real name Kim Hyung-soo) is a South Korean ballad singer under Starship Entertainment. He officially debuted on March 6, 2007, with the studio album Left Heart and its title track “Left Heart,” produced by Park Jin-young.

Known for his emotive ballads and frequent OST appearances, K.Will earned acclaim with hits like “Love 119,” “I Need You,” “Please Don’t…,” “Talk Love,” and “Beautiful Moment”. He initially trained under Bang Si-hyuk and even worked as a guide vocalist for major artists before debut.
