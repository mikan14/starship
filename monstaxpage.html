MONSTA X

MONSTA X is a South Korean boy group formed by Starship Entertainment through the survival show *NO.MERCY*. They officially debuted on May 14, 2015, with the EP *TRESPASS* and its title track of the same name.

Known for their intense performances, powerful choreography, and EDM-hip-hop infused tracks, MONSTA X rose to fame with hits like “HERO,” “DRAMARAMA,” “Shoot Out,” “Follow,” and “Love Killa.” They also achieved global recognition, becoming one of the few K-pop boy groups to tour extensively in the U.S.
